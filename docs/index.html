      The 500

      # Card Set Price 24h

      The S&Poké 500 is a price-weighted index of the 500 most valuable English, raw (ungraded) Pokémon cards, rebased to 1,000 at launch. It moves with the combined market price of the basket — up when cards get pricier, down when they cool.

      Prices from pokemontcg.io (TCGplayer market data), refreshed daily. Not financial advice — for fun and market curiosity.
