      # Card Set Price Daily change

      The S&Poké 500 is a price-weighted index of the 500 most valuable English, raw (ungraded) Pokémon trading cards, rebased to 1,000 at launch. It moves with the combined market price of the basket — up when cards get pricier, down when the market cools. Prices are TCGplayer market data via tcgcsv.com, refreshed daily. Each card is priced by its regular printing (1st Edition printings are out of scope — they trade too thinly on TCGplayer for reliable daily pricing).
